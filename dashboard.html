UK & China Focus

Dedicated analysis of UK and Chinese EV manufacturers. New scenarios calibrated from SMMT 2024, BYD Annual Report 2023, and IEA GEO 2024.

🇬🇧 UK EV Manufacturers

JLR (Tata Motors), BMW MINI Oxford, Vauxhall (Stellantis Ellesmere Port). 175 k EV units/yr (SMMT 2024). Post-Brexit rules-of-origin threshold rises to 55% UK/EU content by 2027 — creating acute sourcing pressure.

🇨🇳 Chinese EV Manufacturers

Split into BYD (1,575 k BEV, 95% vertical integration, 95% self-supplied Blade LFP cells) and Other Chinese OEMs (6,825 k — SAIC, Geely, NIO, Xpeng, Li Auto, GAC — 55% CATL-dependent). CATL concentration risk is the key differentiator.

UK Supply Chain Friction — Production Impact

Post-Brexit border delays + rules-of-origin compliance. −10% throughput yr 1, fading to −5% yr 2. Harness delivery delays modelled separately.

CATL Disruption — Cell Supply Impact

−45% CATL output for 65 weeks (Ningde cluster scenario). Shows cost of 37% global cell supply concentration in one maker.
